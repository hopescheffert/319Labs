Welcome to Drawing Board!

Enter some commands to draw shapes.

Hints:
To make a circle: draw circle [center of x coordinate] [center of y coordinate] [radius of circle] ;
To make a rectangle: draw rectangle [width] [height] ;
To make a line: draw line [start x] [start y] [end x] [end y] ;
To make an ellipse: draw ellipse [center of x coordinate] [center of y coordinate] [radius of x] [radius of y] ;
