Drawing Board Syntax

Each command must end with a semicolon ';'

DRAW

To make a circle: draw circle [id] [center of circle's x coordinate] [center of circle's y coordinate] [radius of circle] ;
Example: draw circle c 30 30 20;
To make a rectangle: draw rectangle [id] [width] [height] ;
Example: draw rectangle r 30 50;
To make a line: draw line [id] [start x] [start y] [end x] [end y] ;
Example: draw line l 5 5 20 20;
To make an ellipse: draw ellipse [id] [center of ellipse's x coordinate] [center of ellipse's y coordinate] [radius of x] [radius of y] ;
Example: draw ellipse e 30 30 20 20;

FILL

fill [shape] [id] [color]
Available colors: red, green, blue, yellow, orange, purple, black
Example: fill circle c green;

MOVE

move [shape] [id] [x coordinate to move to] [y coordinate to move to]
Example: move circle c 50 50;

ROTATE

rotateSelection [shape] [id] [degrees (between 0-360)]
Example: rotate circle c 45;
Be aware: you may rotate the shape out of the svg (grey box) view

GROW

grow [shape] [id]
Example: grow circle c;

SHRINK

shrink [shape] [id]
Exampe: shrink circle c;
